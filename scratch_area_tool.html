Wound Healing Scratch Area (Automatic Front Detection) — Author: Wukun (Kenny) Ouyang, Cornell University

1. Upload microscopy image

Or drag & drop one or more images here

2. Set scale bar

Click the two endpoints of the scale bar (e.g. "100 µm") to get its pixel length.

3. Compute scratch area

The tool will threshold the image (Otsu) and detect the upper and lower cell fronts automatically. Pure white and pure black pixels are always excluded when computing the threshold (e.g. to avoid bias from scale bars).
Define a scale bar on one image, then run batch analysis to apply the same scale to all loaded images.
